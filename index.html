About Me

I'm a skilled operations and finance professional who has decided to take on a new challenge and pursue his passion for front-end development. With my strong analytical and problem-solving skills, I'm well-equipped to excel in this field. I'm currently honing my technical abilities in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create visually stunning and responsive websites.I'm committed to delivering high-quality work that meets and exceeds client expectations while maintaining strict attention to detail. I'm eager to leverage my diverse experience and skills to create impactful and engaging web experiences for users.

I bring a wealth of transferrable skills to my new career in front-end development. My experience in operations and finance has equipped me with a keen eye for detail, a strong work ethic, and an ability to work collaboratively with colleagues and stakeholders. Additionally, I have a passion for user experience (UX) design and wireframing. I understands the importance of creating intuitive and engaging user interfaces that enhance the user experience. My UX design and wireframing skills allow me to approach development projects with a user-centered mindset, ensuring that the final product is not only visually stunning but also functional and user-friendly.
